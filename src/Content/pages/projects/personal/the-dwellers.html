A voxel-art RPG game with tile-based dungeons.

The Dwellers was my final-major-project for my final year studying at Northbrook College. For this project, I worked with the 3D Artist, Nerice, who provided the models and animations for the characters, along with the particle effects for the spells and environments.

Modular Behaviours

Having recently completed the "Phase 2" of my modular behaviour system, I decided that I would like to move onto making a node-based solution for modular behaviours.

The Dwellers implemented my "Phase 3" node-based modular behaviour system for item behaviours.
